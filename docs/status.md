# Project Status

Open Audio Tools is a side project — I contribute as much time as I can but have other priorities at this moment and looking forward to being able to spend more time and start building things. The goal is to reach a working prototype by theend of 2026.

**[Project Board](https://github.com/orgs/openaudiotools/projects/2)** · **[Journal](journal/README.md)**

At the moment the devices are practicly in concept or early design phase. I'm hoping/waiting for your feedback and input. Constructive critique is more then welcome even the harsh one,as long as it comes with a "why" and "what if". There are many things I don't know and want to learn. And there is a lot we can achieve together!

## What's next?

I will be slowly working on [SynTEE](https://github.com/openaudiotools/syntee) as the core of the system, or more like it's main unit. I will also track and keep looking for people interrested in contributing.

## Phases

- Concept - A bit more than an idea, the general funciotn and sketch of a device, the "WHY"
- Product Design - fleshing out how it will work and look, first BOM sketch, the "WHAT"
- Technical Design - BOM, PCBs, enclosure designs, documentation, the "HOW"
- Prototype - Putting it somewhat together from first pcb and enclosure prints, the "WTH"
- Kit - finding distribution and sourcing parts for kits, - the "Hell YEAH!"

Innitiatives can move forth and back the phases. Things happen, things get rewritten. Well up untill kits are ready :)
